# Findings & Decisions: ArtisanFlow

## Requirements
- Target: Artisans (plumbers, electricians, painters, etc.)
- Core Value: Simplicity, Speed, Desktop-First (PC)

- Features: 
  - Quote creation (single page, simple fields)
  - Automatic calculations (Tax, Total)
  - PDF Generation
  - Quote to Invoice conversion
  - Online payment (Stripe)
  - Electronic signature (Tactile)
  - Payment tracking

## Research Findings
- Existing Stitch Project: `projects/14396422046133173455` (Artisan Gestion Simplifiée)
- Design Theme: "The Architectural Craftsman" (Asymmetry, Tonal Layering, No-Line Rule).

## Technical Decisions
| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Next.js App Router | Modern, standard for SaaS with Supabase |
| Supabase | Required backend for Auth, DB, and Realtime |
| Stripe | Required for payments |
| Desktop-First | Primary use case for artisans managing business via PC |
